ABOUT US

Acharya Prafulla Chandra Ray Polytechnic, a diploma level Government Technical Institute named after the famous Indian scientist Acharaya Prafulla Chandra Ray (2 August 1861 – 16 June 1944). It was established long back at 1950 and formerly known as Jadavpur Polytechnic. It is located at 188, Raja Subodh Chandra Mallick Road, Jadavpur, Kolkata, West Bengal - 700032. It is affiliated to the West Bengal State Council for Technical and Vocational Education and approved by All India Council for Technical Education (AICTE).
Welcome to the Official Website of Department of Computer Science and Technology, Acharyya Prafulla Chandra Ray Polytechnic, Jadavpur. Our Department is a Pure Technological Department delivering various tools,softwares and awareness of Cyber Security.
